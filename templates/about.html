How Our AI Works

1. Image Processing

Our system uses advanced computer vision algorithms to preprocess and analyze uploaded images, extracting key features that indicate plant health status.

2. Feature Extraction

The AI model identifies patterns in leaf color, texture, spots, and other visual indicators that are characteristic of specific diseases.

3. Disease Classification

Using transfer learning with MobileNetV2, our model classifies the detected patterns into specific disease categories with confidence scores.

4. Treatment Recommendations

Based on the identified disease, our system provides targeted treatment recommendations and prevention strategies from our comprehensive database.
